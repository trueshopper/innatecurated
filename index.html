Everything on this site is something I've actually looked into. I check the ingredient list, I look at the research, and I don't recommend things just because they're popular or because the commission is good.

OUR CRITERIA

THE STANDARD

CLEAN INGREDIENTS

No artificial dyes, unnecessary fillers, or flow agents. Nothing linked to hormone disruption or inflammation. I check every ingredient — not just the headline ones.

TRANSPARENT LABELING

No proprietary blends. I want to see exactly what's in it and exactly how much. If a brand is hiding behind a "blend," I don't trust them.

THIRD-PARTY TESTED

NSF, Informed Sport, or USP only. I need an independent lab to have confirmed the label actually matches what's inside the bottle.

EVIDENCE-BASED

The ingredient needs actual research behind it, at a dose that does something. Not trend ingredients added for marketing.

NO GREENWASHING

"Natural" and "clean" mean nothing without proof. I look at the actual ingredient list, not the packaging claims.

REAL EFFICACY

Does the mechanism make sense? Is it dosed properly? Does the research support the claim being made? If not, it's out.

If something doesn't pass every single one of these, it doesn't go on this site. Doesn't matter how popular it is, how good the marketing is, or what the commission looks like.
